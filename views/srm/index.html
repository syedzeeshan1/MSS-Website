We are the student body technical community at Muffakham Jah College of Engineering and Technologhy, a recognized students club by Microsoft.

About MSSMicrosoft Student Society

The Microsoft Student Society is a place where students can unite, build and grow. This Society will serve as a platform for the students to build their network and enhance their technical skills by utilising all the latest technologies that Microsoft has to offer.

Our aim is to give exposure to all our members and help them strengthen In their field of expertise. In order to achieve so the MSS provides a well-established network through which interaction between various Microsoft communities has been made possible.

The Microsoft Student Society is an initiative taken to empower the students of today to create the world of tomorrow.

About MSPMicrosoft Student Partner Programme

The Microsoft Student Partners is a worldwide recognizable program to sponsor undergraduate and postgraduate students majoring in disciplines related to technology, typically computer science, computer information systems, and information technology.

MSP program aims to enhance students' employability and increase students' awareness of Microsoft technologies.

Student Partners are given access to the latest Microsoft software, development tools, reference material, industry events, and training opportunities. MSPs further share the knowledge among the academic community by arranging courses; giving presentations and lectures; and initiating projects among the students.

Student Partners are offered training especially in product-specific skills not typically taught in academia. The program aims to increase awareness of Microsoft products, programs and initiatives. Consequently, the program helps expanding the user base of Microsoft products, and results in better availability of properly educated workforce in those technologies.
