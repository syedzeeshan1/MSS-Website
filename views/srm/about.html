We believe in spreading awareness across
Hyderbad.

Microsoft gave us an opportunity to link our college with one of the world’s leading firms. We the students of Muffakham Jah College of Engineering and Technologhy formed ’Microsoft Student Society’ to publish information about the new upcoming technologies in the market. We don’t restrict ourselves to the college campus only. We believe in spreading awareness across Hyderabad, thus we conduct workshops and seminars in the city as well.

Our workshops are open for all to attend and the audience can have a personal interactive session with the panel members. We the members of the Society believe in shaping our nation in the blanket of growing technology and upgrade our position in the world ranking of most technological countries and we thank Microsoft for providing us with the opportunity to do so.
